A mobile app for international students by offering resources, support, and community-building features. The app's purpose is to support students in overcoming the obstacles of studying abroad and maximizing their experience in a foreign country.

Overview

The Problem

International students often struggle to find the resources and support they need to succeed in a new country. They face a variety of challenges, including navigating a new academic and cultural environment, and finding social connections.

The Solution

A mobile app that can provide international students with an all-inclusive and accessible resources, support, and community-building opportunities to help them successfully navigate the challenges of studying abroad and make the most of their time in a new country.

Why create an app for International Students?

  • After gaining insight into what motivated students to study abroad, significant pain points were identified which was crucial in helping develop a design solution that would address those needs. .
  • Research showed that International students struggled with adapting to a new culture.
  • Finding housing and financial support is a difficult process especially for International students.
  • International students need more resources and support when it comes to accessing social services.
  • All of the users interviewed felt that isolation and loneliness was a major struggle they faced when integrating into a new country.
